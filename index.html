A long time ago in a galaxy far,
far away...

You are a Jedi. Crisis is about to strike. Unless you can save the galaxy in time, there will be nothing left.

Travel around the galaxy in order to save us all and complete your mission. At each stop, you have the option of attempting a special mission before moving on to the next location. Remember, special missions are dangerous, and will not always work in your favor.

    • Open the Console:

    1. Define your name:
    var YOU = new Jedi("YOURNAME");

    2. Travel:
    YOU.flyTo(LOCATION);

    3. Optional special mission:
    YOU.tryMission();
