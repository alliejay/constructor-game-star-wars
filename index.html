A long time ago in a galaxy far,
far away...

The Imperial Empire has once again tightened it's grip on the galaxy. After many years of growing their arsenal and resources, they are now ready to begin their final assault. If they are not stopped, soon all life will succumb to their will.

Unbeknownst to all, an ancient order of warriors who fought for peace and justice in the time of the Old Republic have been living in the shadows. You are a product of this ancient order and the last hope to stop the evil empire once and for all.

You must travel around the galaxy in an effort to weed out the Imperial scum. At each location you have the ability to deploy from the main station onto each planet. You'll need to seek out natives for assistance with our mission.

You'll also have the option of attempting a Jedi quest. If successful, you will empower the Jedi Rebellion and increase our odds of defeating the Imperial Galactic Empire.
